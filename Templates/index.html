About the CDMX Risk Atlas

Data

This dashboard presents in a simple way the degrees and/or indices of danger and vulnerability to natural disasters in CDMX at the dictrict level. The indicators were calculated by CENAPRED and the data is available in Datos abiertos CDMX.

The indices presented in this dashboard are:

  • Precipitation: it measures ...

The colors represents the level of risk where:

  • Red: represents ...
  • White: represents ...

For a National Risks Atlas you can look at the Mexican Government dashboard Indicadores Municipales de Peligro, Exposicion y Vulnerabilidad, where you can find the indicators by municipality.

Methodology

To see how this dashboard was built you can visit our Github Repository Disasters-mxcity, where you will find the html, css, javascript, data and app.

How to use the dashboard

In this dashboard you can select:

  • Indices per AGEB: select a layer in the map
  • A comparison between districts per indice
  • A comparison of indice per district
